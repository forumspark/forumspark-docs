# Notifications

Notifications keep you informed about activity on the board that is relevant to you.

[[toc]]

## Notification types

You may receive notifications for the following events:

- **Topic reply** — someone replied to a topic you are watching.
- **Mention** — another member mentioned you in a post using `@yourusername`.
- **Reaction** — someone reacted to one of your posts.
- **Private message** — you received a new private message.
- **Achievement unlocked** — you earned an achievement.

## Viewing notifications

Click the notification icon in the navigation bar to see your recent notifications. Each entry shows a summary of the event, who triggered it and a link to the relevant content.

## Managing notification preferences

You can control which notifications you receive by visiting **Edit Profile** and navigating to the notification settings. The following options are available:

- **Email on topic reply** — receive an email when someone replies to a watched topic.
- **Weekly digest** — receive a compiled summary of board activity each week.
- **Auto-watch replies** — automatically watch any topic you reply to.

## Watching topics

When you watch a topic, you receive a notification each time someone posts a new reply. You automatically watch topics that you create. If the **Auto-watch replies** option is enabled, you will also watch topics that you reply to.

To manually watch or unwatch a topic, click the **Watch** / **Unwatch** button at the top of the topic page.
