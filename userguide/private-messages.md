# Private Messages

Private messages let you have one-to-one conversations with other members away from the public forums.

[[toc]]

## Starting a conversation

To send a private message, visit a member's profile and click **Message**. If you have an existing conversation with that member, it will be opened. Otherwise, a new conversation is created.

![Start Conversation](/screenshots/userguide/start-conversation.png)

## Sending messages

Type your message in the text box at the bottom of the conversation and click **Send**. Messages support BBCode formatting and must be between 5 and 5,000 characters.

## Viewing your conversations

Your conversations are listed in your message inbox, showing the other member's name and a preview of the most recent message. Unread conversations are highlighted.

Click on any conversation to view the full message history. Older messages are loaded automatically as you scroll up.

![Conversations](/screenshots/userguide/conversations.png)

## Blocking

If you have blocked a user (or they have blocked you), you will not be able to send messages to each other.
